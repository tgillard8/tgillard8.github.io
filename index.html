Top 5 Netflix Genres and Random Film Selector

How to Use

To interact with the visualizations:

  • Click on any bar in the "Top 5 Genres by Number of Films" chart to see details of a randomly selected film from that genre.
  • Click the "Pick a Random Genre" button to randomly select a genre and display details of a randomly selected film from that genre.
  • Click the "View Box Plot Visualization" button to navigate to the box plot visualization page.
